Your favorite brunch spot in Cyprus. Fresh food, cozy vibes, and unforgettable flavors.


See Menu

Edem’s Yard is a medium-sized hospitality business located in the center of Larnaca. It was founded with the goal of offering exceptional customer experiences through a menu that uniquely blends traditional and modern cuisine. Beyond its distinctive dishes, the restaurant stands out for its creative interior design, which has made it one of the most popular brunch destinations in Cyprus, attracting both locals and tourists.

The business is co-owned and managed by two partners who handle all major decisions. However, a significant issue has emerged regarding the fair distribution of ownership shares between them, especially considering that the business is intended to be passed on to their children in the future to ensure its continued success.
